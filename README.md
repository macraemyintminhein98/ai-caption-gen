# Captiona AI

Captiona AI is an AI-powered Instagram caption generator designed to help small business owners, influencers, social media managers, and individuals create engaging captions with ease. 

Simply input your keywords/themes and select your niche, and our AI will generate several caption options for you. Never run out of ideas for your Instagram posts again!

## Features:
- AI-powered caption generation
- Niche-specific caption optimization
- User-friendly interface
